Curried carrot Soup

Rating:

Reviews: (47)

3.491 Shares

Michael Caines

Ingredients

  • 500g unsalted butter

  • 150g chopped onions

  • 2 crushed garlic cloves

  • 500g peeled and chopped carrots

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

  • ½ tsp Madras curry powder

  • 300ml chicken stock (or vegetable stock)

  • 1 bouquet garni

  • 1tbsp freshly chopped coriander to serve

  • Salt

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a pan and sweat the onion, garlic and carrots with a pinch of salt, until softened.

  2. Meanwhile toast the cumin seeds in a dry frying pan until fragrant. Then add the cumin seeds and curry powder to the vegetables, and cook for 2-3 minutes.

  3. Add the stock, bouquet garni and 500ml water, and bring to boil. Reduce to simmer and cook for 30 minutes.

  4. Remove the bouquet garni and blend the soup until smooth. Pass through a sieve and season to taste with salt and pepper.
